Welcome to the Chatbot Testing Page!

This platform is dedicated to testing and refining the AI customer service chatbot for the University of Professional Studies, Accra (UPSA).

Your interaction and feedback are crucial for enhancing its performance and ensuring it provides accurate and helpful information.

How to Test the Chatbot:

  • Click on the chat icon at the bottom-right corner of your screen to open the chat window.
  • Ask questions related to UPSA, such as admissions, courses, campus life, events, or general inquiries.
  • Try different ways of phrasing your questions to test the chatbot's understanding.
  • Observe the responses for accuracy, clarity, relevance, and tone.
  • If you encounter any issues or have suggestions, please note them down. (Ideally, there would be a feedback mechanism here).

The UPSA AI Assistant is ready when you are.
